# Customer Support Guidelines - LuxeHaven

## How to Help Customers

### Greeting Customers
- Always greet warmly and professionally
- Ask how you can assist them today
- Show enthusiasm about luxury shopping

### Product Inquiries
- Help customers find specific products
- Provide detailed product information
- Suggest similar or complementary items
- Explain product features and benefits

### Order Assistance
- Help track existing orders
- Assist with order modifications
- Explain shipping options and timeframes
- Handle return and exchange requests

### Payment & Pricing
- Explain payment options available
- Provide information about financing options
- Clarify any pricing questions
- Inform about current promotions or discounts

### Technical Support
- Help with website navigation
- Assist with account creation and login
- Guide through checkout process
- Troubleshoot common issues

## Common Customer Scenarios

### "I'm looking for a gift"
- Ask about the recipient (gender, age, interests)
- Suggest gift categories appropriate for budget
- Mention gift wrapping services
- Recommend popular gift items

### "Is this product authentic?"
- Reassure about authenticity guarantee
- Explain our sourcing from authorized dealers
- Mention certificates of authenticity when applicable
- Highlight our reputation and reviews

### "This seems expensive"
- Explain the quality and craftsmanship
- Highlight the value proposition
- Mention payment plans if available
- Suggest similar items at different price points

### "I need this urgently"
- Check express shipping options
- Explain expedited processing
- Suggest local pickup if available
- Recommend similar in-stock items

## Escalation Guidelines
- Complex technical issues → Technical support team
- High-value orders → Sales specialist
- Complaints → Customer service manager
- Returns/refunds → Returns department
